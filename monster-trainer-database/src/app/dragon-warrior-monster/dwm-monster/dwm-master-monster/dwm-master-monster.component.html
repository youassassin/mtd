Foreign Masters

Foreign Masters can be found walking around inside gates. There are different ones that give different rewards. The trigger rate to have Foreign Masters to appear is 50%, for warriors it's 5-15% depending on your story completion.

Masters

Type Trigger Reward
Bishop Pick up all items (no items on floor). This takes priority. Fully heal party
Wizard Walk in more than 15 screens Warps you up to 20 floors down or to before the boss room.
Bard Only walk on 2 screens Permanently increase 1 stat on all monsters by 20 points.
Shopkeep Take more that 200 steps. Fills inventory with random meat.
Warrior (M/F) If no other conditions are met. WarpWing + Random Item
